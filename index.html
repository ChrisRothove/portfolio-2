This is Me

I'm Chris, a software engineer living in Kansas City Missouri. I consider myself a student of many different disciplines, and this makes me uniquely understanding of various endeavors and walks of life. It sounds like a fancy way of saying I'm a jack of all trades, and it is. But my focus is, and always will be, trying to reach people with stories that unite us as human beings.

This is My Story

I came from an art background. The biggest thing I learned from the Kansas City Art Institute was that I didn't want a career in art. I have always enjoyed coding with HTML and CSS recreationally, and joined Thinkful to refine those skills into a new, more fulfilling career.

I'm excited to work with other developers on complex projects, and hope to design apps and websites which will reach people in new and interesting ways. I love to tell stories, and I want the things I work on to become a part of the stories of other people.

When not coding I like to play and design videogames, play Dungeons and Dragons with my friends and play collectible card games.

My Projects

Mirage Masters

Mirage Masters is a personal project for a card game I'm working on for my online friends. It has a small audience and is not meant to be a profitable endeavor, but it is a fun way to keep the things I am learning relevant to me and impress my friends. One day soon I would like it to be able to function as a manual for the card game. In time I'd like to add a card game look-up, and a way to simulate card pulls from packs. Eventually I'll have a small database of cards to pull from.

this site uses event listeners, sticky header, and hide navigation.
